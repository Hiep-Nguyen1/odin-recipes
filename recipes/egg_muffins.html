Egg Muffins

Description

A base template for delicious egg muffins that can have endless variations. These are convenient as a meal prep idea for those that are in a rush in the morning.

Ingredients

  • 10 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup of cream or milk
  • 1-2 cups of fillings for the muffins
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter

Special Equipment

  • 12 Muffin baking pan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and adjust rack to the center.
  2. Whisk together eggs and milk until fully incorporated. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Using a stick of cold butter, grease the muffin tins
  4. Add fillings into each tin and add egg mixture until even with the top. Stir slightly to evenly incorporate mixture.
  5. Place in oven and allow to cook for 13-15 minutes, until eggs are slightly puffed and small bits of golden areas appear.
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes and enjoy or store inside the fridge in a container.
