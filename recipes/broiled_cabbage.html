Broiled Cabbage

Description

A quick, delicious and simple side dish to any meal. All it takes is a few minutes under a broiled to get the sweet, nutty and earthy notes that cabbage develops when slightly charred.

Ingredients

  • 1 quarter head of Cabbage
  • 3-4 tbsp of olive oil

Steps

  1. Set your oven to broil on high and adjust rack for the top of the oven.
  2. Cut your cabbage into 1-inch thick slices.
  3. Spread out evenly on a baking sheet pan and add oil, salt and pepper.
  4. Place baking sheet pan on the highest rack and cook until slightly charred, roughly 5 minutes.
